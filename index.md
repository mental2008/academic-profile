## About Me

I am currently a senior engineer in the TRE (Tech Infra and Reliability Engineering) team at Alibaba Group, working on AI infrastructure. I am also a joint postdoctoral researcher with [Alibaba DAMO Academy](https://damo.alibaba.com/) and [Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU)](https://www.sjtu.edu.cn/). Before that, I received my Ph.D. degree in [Department of Computer Science and Engineering](https://www.cse.ust.hk/) from [Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST)](http://www.ust.hk/) and my B.Eng. degree in [School of Computer Science and Engineering](http://www.scut.edu.cn/cs/) from [South China University of Technology (SCUT)](https://www.scut.edu.cn/).

My current research interests center on building **agentic AI infrastructure**, including (but not limited to): leveraging agents to improve infrastructure efficiency, enabling agent-driven workload adaptation across heterogeneous GPUs, and designing AI infrastructure that is natively friendly to agents.
